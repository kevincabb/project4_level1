About:

Michael’s Pizza has been located at 2300 West Alpine Avenue for 28 years. We have such a loyal customer base that we are so thankful for. We are thankful everyday for each and every one of our customers. We pride ourselves on customer service. All of our pizzas are made to order and are made with the freshest ingredients. Our neighborhood has always supported our business. We have seen the neighborhood evolve. New families are starting to move in with their families and the neighborhood cycle continues. We are truly lucky.

Michael's Pizza has been family owned and operated since November 17, 1978. We are happy to be a part of Stockton and San Joaquin County. Our pizza has been voted number one year after year. We start by making all of our dough and pizza sauce. We take great pride in our home-made pizzas. All of our toppings are cut fresh daily and no pizza toppings are aloud to go on our pizzas if they are not fresh. That goes for all of our food at Michael’s Pizza. All lettuce for our salads is cut fresh, as well as our salad dressings.

Our cafe is always serving great coffee and specialty drinks and fresh baked goods are made and baked right in the cafe. We have an awesome staff that is always here to welcome you on those early, cold, blustery mornings with hot refreshments.
Additionally, all of our coffees and specialty drinks can be made hot or cold. We have healthy smoothies, yogurt, and gelato. You always need something sweet after a great meal and the cafe is a great choice! Try even for breakfast! You will not be disappointed.

It is always a pleasure to see a family come into Michael’s Pizza and enjoy a great meal that is reasonably priced. The kids get to play in our gaming area and the HDTV’s are great for the adults to watch all of the hottest games.

We believe that customer service is number one and we value every customer that we have. That is why we are number one and plan on being number one for many years to come. Stop by and see us soon, it will be great to see you again!
